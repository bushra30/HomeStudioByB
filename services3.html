Interior Decoration and Room Refresh

Looking to refresh a room without a major overhaul? Let us transform your space with minimal effort and maximum impact.

Book a Call →
Our Room Refresh service offers an easy way to revitalize your space without extensive renovation. From personalized mood boards to curated decor, we provide everything you need for a cohesive update. Ideal for spaces that need a fresh look, whether it's a cozy living room, a fun kids' room, or a sophisticated dining area.

What's Included:

  • Initial Consultation: Getting to know your style and goals for the room.
  • 3D Renderings: Visualizing the new look before committing.
  • Mood Boards & Color Scheme Development: Inspiring design concepts and color palettes.
  • Shopping List for Decor & Furniture: Curated items tailored to your room's needs.
  • Final Styling & Contractor Coordination Tips: Adding the perfect accessories and finishing touches to elevate your space, with guidance on contractor coordination if installation is required.

By choosing HomeStudioByB's services, you can transform your space into a beautifully designed, functional, and personalized environment that you'll love experiencing every day.
