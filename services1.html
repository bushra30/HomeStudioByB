Design Consultation

Have a vision for your space but need guidance and reliable support to bring it to life? We offer expert design and smooth contractor coordination to turn your ideas into a stunning, functional reality.

Book a Call →
Perfect for those looking to transform their space with professional design guidance and coordinated support. Our Design Consultation service includes everything from material selection to layout planning, with expert recommendations to ensure a polished outcome. We work closely with you and provide the resources needed to coordinate with contractors, helping your vision come together effortlessly.

What's Included:

Initial Consultation: Understanding your vision, preferences, and requirements for your home.

  • Material and Finish Selection: Advising on the best materials (including tiles, floors, cabinets, vanities and many more)and finishes for durability and aesthetic appeal.
  • 3D Renderings: Providing detailed visuals and moodboards.
  • Space Planning & Layout Development: Maximizing flow and functionality across every room.
  • Project Management Guidance & Contractor Coordination: Supporting you with timelines, budgets, and contractor coordination to ensure smooth implementation.
  • Furniture & Decor Selection: Recommending pieces that enhance both style and comfort.
  • Final Styling: Providing finishing touches to complete your space.

By choosing HomeStudioByB's services, you can transform your space into a beautifully designed, functional, and personalized environment that you'll love experiencing every day.
